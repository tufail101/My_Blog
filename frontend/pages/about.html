About BlogSite

BlogSite is a modern full-stack platform where you can read and write blogs, share your thoughts, and engage with a like-minded community. Built with HTML, CSS, Tailwind CSS, MySQL, and Express, it offers seamless user authentication and a robust blogging experience.

Users can sign up, log in, manage their profiles, write their own blogs, read others' posts, and participate in discussions. BlogSite is designed to provide a simple, elegant, and interactive platform for sharing knowledge and experiences.
